The share price started at 150 pence in January and fell slightly to
130 in February. It plummeted to 120 in March, but grew steadily in
April and May to reach a bit less than 130. It climbed up to 135 in June
and remained at that level until August when it soared to 170 ‒ the
year’s peak. The price fell slightly in September to mark at a bit
less than 170 in October. It fell further in November to 160 and then
plummeted to 130 in December.
